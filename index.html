About Me



I am a Facilities Engineer with hands on experience in building autonomous systems.
I am skilled in Python, Data Visualization, JavaScript, C++, Arduino coding, as well as CAD designing with Fusion 360, SolidWorks, and AutoCAD.

The next realms of technology that I am eager to explore are Artificial Intelligence and product management.
My goal is to improve the service quality while ensuring cost- and time-efficiency
as well as sustainable production and transport processes within the supply network of companies.

In addition, I am a spirited advocate for underrepresented minorities in STEM.
I volunteer as a Youth Career Connect mentor for NYC Department Of Education and
founded Society of Women Engineers chapter on my campus to promote diversity and inclusion.
